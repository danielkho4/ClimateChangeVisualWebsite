The Decline of Glacier Area

Glacier mass has declined sharply since the start of the 1945. Glaciers provide water during the summer months when they melt. The cold runoff water is home to a diverse set of plants and animals, without the resource would not exist.

The Rise of Sea Level

With the melting of glaciers, sea levels have increased steadily since the 1800s. Many experts intuitively attribute this to an effect of the rising temperatures and the rising levels of CO2, as well as other Greenhouse Gases in our atmosphere. As sea levels rise, we will see more and more of an effect our coastline habitats.
